Accountability

When you give to Empower Dreams, 100% of your donation is applied to those projects that grab your heart.

Every cent of your donation is applied to the project of your choice. The board of directors personally fund 100% of the organization's overhead expenses in order to multiply the effectiveness of your giving.

Additionally, Empower Dreams comes alongside organizations that are already producing fruit and consequently helps take their results to the next level.

The board of directors builds personal relationships with all the partner organizations and personally visits these projects to ensure best practices accountability.
