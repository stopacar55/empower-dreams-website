Vision February 28, 2023

Brent and Kris Share the Exciting Refined Focus for the Future

As Empower Dreams has grown and matured, we’ve been prayerfully refining our focus to maximize our impact for the Kingdom. Here’s what we’re excited about going forward.

Over the past several years, God has been teaching us where our unique strengths lie and where your generosity can have the greatest eternal impact. We’ve identified key areas of focus that align with both our mission and the greatest needs in the world.

Our Refined Focus

  • Identifying highly effective discipleship and evangelism partnerships around the world
  • Nurturing personal relationships with every partner — with regular visits to the field
  • Resourcing proven partners who are already bearing fruit — pouring fuel on the fire

What Sets Empower Dreams Apart

We remain committed to the principles that have defined us from day one:

  • 100% of donations go directly to ministry projects
  • Personal relationships with every partner organization
  • Culturally compelling approaches, especially for oral cultures
  • Accountability through regular reporting and field visits

Looking Ahead

We are more excited than ever about what God is doing through Empower Dreams. From Bible translations to discipleship movements, from famine relief to training indigenous leaders — every project we fund is carefully selected for maximum eternal impact.

You were made for more. Come join us and see what God is doing through your faithful generosity.

Thank you for being part of this journey with us.

— Brent and Kris
