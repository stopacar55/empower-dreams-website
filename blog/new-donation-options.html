Great New Empower Dreams Donation Options

We are thrilled to announce two exciting new ways to support the work of Empower Dreams. Giving has never been easier, and every dollar you contribute goes directly to the projects you care about most.

First, Empower Dreams has been approved as a registered Facebook Charity. This means you can now donate directly through Facebook, set up fundraisers on our behalf, and even designate Empower Dreams as a beneficiary for your birthday fundraiser. Facebook covers all processing fees, so 100% of your gift reaches our projects.

Second, we have partnered with Donorbox, a trusted and secure online payment service that makes recurring and one-time donations simple and convenient. Donorbox supports credit cards, debit cards, ACH bank transfers, and even PayPal, giving you the flexibility to give however works best for you.

As always, when you give to Empower Dreams, every cent of your donation is applied to the project of your choice. The board of directors personally fund 100% of overhead expenses, so your generosity has maximum impact. We are grateful for these new tools that make it even easier for you to change lives around the world.
