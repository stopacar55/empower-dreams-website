Audio Bibles Are Transforming Lives Worldwide

Audio Bibles are transforming lives around the world and spreading the gospel to unreached communities. Empower Dreams trains and equips local leaders who use Audio Bibles to establish listening groups where people encounter God's Word together.

This is the heart of our mission: equipping local leaders with the tools and training they need to bring Scripture to communities where it has never been heard. When you give to Empower Dreams, you fuel that work.

"And he said to them, 'Go into all the world and proclaim the gospel to the whole creation.'"

— Mark 16:15
