Robyn’s Journey from Volunteering to the Board

What began as a simple desire to help has grown into a deep calling. Robyn shares how her journey with Empower Dreams took her from volunteering to serving on the board of directors.

Robyn’s story with Empower Dreams began the way many great stories do — with a willingness to show up. She first connected with the ministry as a volunteer, drawn by the mission to reach the unreached and the unique model that sends every donated dollar directly to the field. What she found was more than an organization — it was a family united by a common purpose.

As she invested more of her time and energy, Robyn began to see the impact up close. She traveled to the field, met partners, and witnessed the transformation happening in communities around the world. Her passion for the work deepened, and it became clear that God was calling her to a greater role within the ministry.

Today, Robyn serves on the Empower Dreams board of directors, bringing her heart for missions, her professional expertise, and her firsthand experience to the leadership of the organization. She is particularly passionate about discipleship in Peru and ensuring that every partnership is built on authentic, personal relationships.

Robyn’s journey is a beautiful reminder that God uses our willingness — no matter how small the first step — to accomplish things far beyond what we could imagine. If you feel a stirring in your heart to get involved, we would love to hear from you.
