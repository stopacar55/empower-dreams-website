Northern Peru Discipleship News

Your willingness to give and pray has been mightily used by the Lord. Ricky and Carol Hendon’s granddaughters are thrilled that your generosity has made Camp Yuri easily accessible for the youth of Peru, enabling them to be discipled and loved.

This accessibility will also allow for greater ministry capacity and results. We hope you’re as excited as we are about what God is doing in Northern Peru through this partnership.

Camp Yuri: A Place of Transformation

Camp Yuri has become a beacon of hope for young people in the region. Through your support, the camp has been improved and made more accessible, allowing more youth to attend discipleship programs and experience the love of Christ in a tangible way.

The Hendon family has been faithfully serving in Peru for years, and this investment in Camp Yuri multiplies their impact exponentially. Every young person who comes through the camp doors has the opportunity to encounter Jesus and be equipped to share their faith with others.

Your generosity has made Camp Yuri easily accessible for the youth of Peru, enabling them to be discipled and loved.

Thank you for your continued investment in the next generation of believers in Peru. To God be the glory!
